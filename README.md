# 🛡️ SurakshaSaathi
### *Your Safety. Your Freedom. Your Financial Independence.*

---

# 📖 Overview

**SurakshaSaathi** is an innovative **Safety + FinTech Super App** designed to empower women by combining **personal safety, financial security, and financial independence** into a single intelligent platform.

Unlike traditional safety applications that only provide SOS alerts or fintech applications that only manage money, SurakshaSaathi integrates **real-time safety assistance, emergency finance, smart payments, evidence collection, and credit building** into one seamless ecosystem.

Whether travelling alone, working as a gig professional, or managing personal finances, SurakshaSaathi acts as a **trusted travel companion and financial guardian**, ensuring women remain both **safe and financially independent**.

---

# 🎯 Problem Statement

Although many applications exist for women's safety and digital payments, they solve only one side of the problem.

Current challenges include:

- 🚨 Safety apps provide SOS alerts but cannot provide emergency financial assistance.
- 💳 FinTech apps manage money but cannot detect unsafe situations or trigger emergency support.
- 💰 Women under financial control cannot secretly save or access emergency funds.
- 🚕 Women gig workers face risks during deliveries, rides, or home services with no integrated emergency response.
- 📉 Women with little financial history struggle to obtain independent credit or micro-loans.
- 📂 After emergencies, collecting evidence like location, audio, videos, and chats becomes difficult, weakening legal cases.

### Core Gap

There is **no unified platform** where **money management, personal safety, and evidence collection work together** to protect women.

---

# 💡 Proposed Solution

SurakshaSaathi combines **Safety Technology + FinTech** into one intelligent application.

The platform provides:

- 🛡️ Personal Safety Features
- 💳 Financial Protection
- 📂 Automatic Evidence Collection
- 📍 Smart Navigation
- 🚨 Silent Emergency Support
- 📈 Independent Credit Building

Instead of treating safety and finance separately, SurakshaSaathi makes financial tools actively contribute to women's safety.

# ✨ Key Features

## 🛡️ Smart Safety Layer

### 📍 Smart Navigation Map

Provides safer navigation by showing:

- Well-lit streets
- Crowdsourced safety ratings
- Nearby police stations
- Nearby hospitals
- Emergency services
- Safer travel routes

---

### 👥 Companion Mode

Users can share:

- Live location
- Route progress
- Estimated arrival time

Automatically alerts emergency contacts if:

- Route changes unexpectedly
- User stops for an unusual duration
- Phone becomes unreachable

---

### 🚨 Discreet SOS

Emergency can be triggered by:

- 📳 Shaking the phone
- 🔊 Pressing the Volume Button three times

The app instantly:

- Sends Silent SOS
- Starts Audio Recording
- Captures Location
- Notifies Emergency Contacts
- Alerts Nearby Police (where supported)

---

## 💳 Financial Independence Layer

### 🏦 Emergency Vault

A hidden emergency wallet with:

- Secret PIN
- Fake PIN Mode
- Hidden emergency savings
- Instant emergency access after SOS

Even if someone forces the user to open the application, emergency funds remain protected.

---

### 💸 SafePay Transaction

During any UPI payment:

If the user taps **"I'm Unsafe"**

The application immediately:

- Freezes the transaction
- Sends Silent SOS
- Shares live location
- Starts evidence recording
- Notifies trusted contacts

---

### 📈 Credit For Her

Creates an independent financial profile using:

- Gig work income
- Savings history
- Digital transaction records
- Emergency vault usage

Benefits include:

- Micro-loans
- Business funding
- Independent credit score
- Financial empowerment

- 📍 Live GPS Location

This information can be securely shared with trusted contacts, law enforcement, or financial institutions when appropriate.
